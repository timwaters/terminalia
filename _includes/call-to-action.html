Happy Terminalia!

Terminalia is a one day festival of walking, space, place and psychogeography on Thursday 23rd Feburary 2016. There will be a number of free events in Leeds and around the world. Terminalia is the festival of Terminus, Roman god of boundaries and landmarks so if there was ever a god and festival for psychogeography this would be it! Events have been run on this day in Leeds since 2011.

Read here for a review by Phil Kirby of a previous walk: The Culture Vulture: A Drink To The City

Open Call for Events

If you want to run a walk or event and want to be associated with the festival, let me know and we will add it to the website! It just needs to be free, psychogeographical, open to the public and on 23rd Feb.

Interventions: The Beatings the Bounds walk has space for a few small interventions along the route and at the stones where participants will stop. Drop me a line and we'll chat.
