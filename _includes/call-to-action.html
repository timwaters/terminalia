Happy Terminalia!

Terminalia is a one day festival of walking, space, place and psychogeography on 23rd Feburary. Terminalia is the festival of Terminus, Roman god of boundaries and landmarks so if there was ever a god and festival for psychogeography this would be it! Events have been run on this day since 2011.

Until the Old Normal returns, the Terminalia Festival of Psychogeography will go underground for 2021. No events will be listed here this year.
The festival is open source - anyone is able to run their own event under the Terminalia Festival of Psychogeography, just go ahead and do it.
