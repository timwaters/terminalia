Happy Terminalia!

Terminalia is a one day festival of walking, space, place and psychogeography on 23rd Feburary. Terminalia was the festival of Terminus, Roman god of boundaries and landmarks! Events have been run on this day since 2011.

Theme: Wisdom. "The ability to discern or judge what is true, right, or lasting. The sum of learning through the ages".

Want to run an event for Terminalia 2022? Contact Tim to add your event here. Events should be psychogeographical, open to anyone, free and ideally in the real world although online events are accepted. Events that are online only should have a good level of participation.
You set up the event, handle the numbers etc and it can show up here.
Private events, responses and any blog posts etc can be submitted after the festival for inclusion.
