Happy Terminalia!

Terminalia is a one day festival of walking, space, place and psychogeography on 23rd Feburary. Terminalia was the festival of Terminus, Roman god of boundaries and landmarks! Events have been run on this day since 2011.

Call for events!
Want to run an event for Terminalia 2023? Contact Tim to add your event here. Events should be psychogeographical, open to anyone and free. Events that are online only should have a good level of participation.
